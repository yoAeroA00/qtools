The loading addresses for loaders for various chipsets
(in hexadecimal format, as specified in the -a key of the 'qdload' program)
are as follows:


NPRG8200   00100000
NPRG9x00   00100000
NPRG9x15   41700000
NPRG6695   00800000 (MDM6200)

ENPRG9x15  2A000000
ENPRG9x00  58020000
ENPRG8200  58020000
ENPRG9x25  F8004000
ENPRG6695  20012000
